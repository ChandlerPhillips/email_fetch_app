About us

This program was created by Chandler Phillips for Facilities Management at Murray State University. The intent of this program is to bridge the TempStick sensors and AIM System. The program will gather all information, sent by the Tempstick sensors, split and save this data, and send to the AIM System. To learn more, contact Chandler Phillips (jphillips31@murraystate.edu).
